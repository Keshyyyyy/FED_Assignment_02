About Us

Our History

We are two brothers who are passionate about developing games and one day we started thinking, how can we gamify a learning process? And so, that led to the making of our own gamified trivia platform, BrainWave

Our Goal

We aim to create a platform where learning is as fun as gaming and to encourage the younger generation to partake in such learning activities in hopes of increasing general knowledge among people by gamifying a learning process and rewarding them appropriately to keep them engaged

How are we funding these projects?

Apart from originating from a wealthy family, we also have numerous different established projects that have been generating substantial income for us to pour into other ideas
